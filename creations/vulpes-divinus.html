Vulpes Divinus

Vulpes Divinus is our founding project — a vast, open-world 2D platformer with advanced combat and an immersive narrative. It is still in the early stages of development.

Investigate the World

Vulpes Divinus takes place in Tekia, a massive land of enchantment and mystery. Created by the incomprehensible Theryx, Tekia was meant to be a perfect work of art, but the jealous Quyryx was overtaken by pride and turned against Theryx, infecting Tekia with corruption. He became Kyryx, Dark Lord of Sin, and he seeks nothing more than to destroy every last trace of truth, goodness, and beauty in Tekia.

Explore Tekia, from its highest mountain peaks, to its eternally autumnal forests, to its darkest secretive depths. The world is brimming with unique locations to explore, with many secrets, allies, and enemies along the way. As you discover secrets, you will find unique items to empower yourself; as you find allies, you will embark on quests to help heal the wounds caused by Kyryx's corruption; as you encounter enemies you will use your natural agility and divine blessings to quell the infection of sin in the land.

Build Your Character

As you progress through the game, choose how you want to engage in combat, whether your prefer to focus on honing your weapons or channeling your divine power. With a variety of weapon archetypes and unique ability sets, you can choose your own play style. Prefer to poison enemies and stay out of harm's way? Focus on the abilities granted by the powerful Karion. Would you rather keep your distance and snipe enemies at range? Track down the Essence of Instinct and become an Airscourge, gaining bonuses to your ranged weapons and improved mobility.

Engage the Narrative

Vulpes Divinus is rich in narrative, having both a primary story objective and also many local, secondary story quests to explore. Interact with characters throughout Tekia learn where they come from, and work with them to progress their part in the story of this hallowed world.
